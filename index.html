EDPIF Day 

(Text sugessions)EDPIF doctoral school organises a meeting on Wednesday, June 20 .This meeting aims to bring 2nd year Doctoral canditates registered with EDPIF together to discuss science and life in this beautiful city .  

Selected volunteer PhD students and researchers will give divulgative talks on interesting subjects.
At the end of the day, there will be a ceremony to award the best talks and posters.
